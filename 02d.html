The Verification Paradox


You tested Rook. The test passed. But:
  • An hour lost to testing is an hour lost to action
  • A test that passes tells you what you already suspected
  • A test that fails might come too late to matter

    • Trust is faster than verification. But verification is safer than trust.
    In this work, you're always trading one for the other.
