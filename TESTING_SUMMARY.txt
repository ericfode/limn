================================================================================
COMPARISON WITH ALTERNATIVES
================================================================================

Compositional Mechanisms in Limn:

+ (Addition/Choice)
  "sol + liq" = solid OR liquid (your choice)
  
* (Interference)
  "sol * liq" = gel, glass, slush (liminal blend)
  
→ (Sequence)  
  "sol → liq" = solid becoming liquid (causality/change)

~ (Superposition)
  "sol ~ liq" = solid-or-liquid (uncertainty, unknown which)

@ (Projection)
  "lov @ fer" = fear-component of love (extraction)

UNIQUE POSITION OF *:
  Creates genuinely emergent, non-compositional meanings
  Perfect for boundary/liminal/transitional concepts
  Enables naming previously inexpressible ideas
