## Grammar Rules Learned

1. **Intersection:** Adjacent words combine by intersection
2. **Order Independence:** Word order doesn't matter for meaning
3. **Operator Scope:** Operators apply to the word immediately following
4. **Grouping:** Use "|" to mark scope boundaries
