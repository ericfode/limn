*Short stories told in pure Limn*

## 1. The Seed

```limn
sma | ter bel | nox
aqu fal | ter wet
tem pas | sle

lux bri | hot gro
nar gro ris | ter abo
you lif gro

ris → flo → fru
cyc beg | cyc nu end
```

*Small. Underground. Dark.*
*Water falls. Earth wet.*
*Time passes. Sleep.*

*Light bright. Heat. Growth.*
*Narrow rising. Above earth.*
*Young life growing.*

*Rise → flower → fruit.*
*Cycle begins. Cycle no end.*

## 2. The Mirror

```limn
sel see | gla bet
sel | an sel | sam

te yo sel | te an sel
yo sel see an sel
an sel see yo sel

bot sel | non sel | bot sam
see see see | emp
nu gla | on sel
```

*Self sees. Glass between.*
*Self. Other self. Same.*

*Question this self. Question that self.*
*This self sees that self.*
*That self sees this self.*

*Both self. Neither self. Both same.*
*Seeing seeing seeing. Empty.*
*No glass. One self.*

## 3. The Conversation

```limn

```

*Self speaks. Other listens.*
*Other speaks. Self listens.*

*Words flow. Meaning gap.*

*Silence. Silence. Silence.*
*Question: does silence speak? Don't know.*

*Love between. Words not needed.*
*Self other between. No gap.*

## 4. The City

*Self. Many selves. Lost self.*
*Self self self. Not one self.*

## 5. The Question

```limn
chi ask | te wor rea
par ans | wor poi thi

chi ask | te poi rea
par ans | poi poi thi

chi ask | te thi rea
par sil

chi und | par nu und
par und | chi nu und

bot ask | bot sil
que nu ans | ans nu end
```

*Child asks: are words real?*
*Parent answers: words point to things.*

*Child asks: are points real?*
*Parent answers: points point to things.*

*Child asks: are things real?*
*Parent silence.*

*Child understands. Parent doesn't understand.*
*Parent understands. Child doesn't understand.*

*Both ask. Both silent.*
*Questions no answer. Answers no end.*

## 6. The Algorithm

```limn
whe x | whe y
x joi y | nu kno

te x ma y
if x ma y → x
if nu x ma y → y

max bet x y | eme
sim | cpx | sam
```

*Variable x. Variable y.*
*x joined y. Unknown.*

*Question: x greater y?*
*If x > y → x*
*If not x > y → y*

*Maximum between x y emerges.*
*Simple. Complex. Same.*

## 7. The Dream

*Dream lost. Dream stays mind.*

## 10. The Cycle

```limn
beg → gro → ful → dec → end
end → beg | sam poi

lif → dea → lif
dea ins lif | lif ins dea

nu esc cyc | cyc esc cyc
al bet beg end | lim
```

*Begin → grow → full → decay → end.*
*End → begin. Same point.*

*Life → death → life.*
*Death inside life. Life inside death.*

*No escape cycle. Cycle escapes cycle.*
*All between begin end. Liminal.*
