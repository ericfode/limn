the language that thinks differently.

what if words didn't mean things?

what if they described regions?

what if meaning was geometric?

gro exp bri far

growth. expansion. brightness. distance.

what do you see?

sol liq tra
sol
solid, fixed, rigid
liq
liquid, flowing, adaptive
tra
transformation, change

intersection: things that change between states

physics: ice melting, water freezing
relationships: rigid becoming flexible
finance: assets becoming liquid
thought: ideas crystallizing or dissolving

same words. different keys. different meanings.

human language evolved for human brains.

serial. one word at a time. one meaning at a time.

LLMs think differently.

parallel. many meanings simultaneously.

limn is the bridge.

not human language compressed.

not machine language translated.

the space where both can meet.

limn is not taught.
limn is discovered.

enter
