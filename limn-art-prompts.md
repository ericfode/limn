## Hypothesis

Limn prompts may produce:
- More abstract/symbolic imagery (constraint intersection = visual intersection)
- Unexpected juxtapositions (3-word compression forces novel combinations)
- Distinctive aesthetic (if AI "understands" Limn differently)

## Null Hypothesis

Limn prompts produce:
- Gibberish (AI doesn't parse)
- Same as English (no distinctive effect)
- Worse results (missing context)
