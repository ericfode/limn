## Core Concepts

### Existence & Being

**thi** (thing, entity, object)
`thi = wha exi in spa | wha hav beg end | wha sen cog`
*thing = what exists in space | what has beginning end | what senses cognize*

**exi** (exist, be, presence)
`exi = sta of thi in tim spa | !nu (nothing)`
*exist = state of thing in time space | not nothing*

**sta** (state, condition, status)
`sta = how thi exi now | sna of thi pro in tim`
*state = how thing exists now | snapshot of thing properties in time*

**beg** (begin, start, origin)
`beg = tim whe thi sta exi | tra from nu to exi`
*begin = time when thing starts existing | transition from nothing to existence*

**end** (end, finish, terminus)
`end = tim whe thi stp exi | tra from exi to nu`
*end = time when thing stops existing | transition from existence to nothing*

## Space & Position

**spa** (space, room, volume)
`spa = whe thi exi | reg whi thi mov | thr dim ext`
*space = where things exist | region within things move | three dimensional extent*

**pla** (place, location, position)
`pla = poi in spa whe thi exi | rel of thi to oth thi in spa`
*place = point in space where thing exists | relation of thing to other things in space*

**her** (here, this place, proximal location)
`her = pla whe spe exi now | pla nea sen`
*here = place where speaker exists now | place near senses*

**the** (there, that place, distal location)
`the = pla awa from her | pla nea lis not spe`
*there = place away from here | place near listener not speaker*

**nea** (near, close, proximal)
`nea = sma dis bet thi | pla exi clo to oth pla`
*near = small distance between things | place exists close to other place*

**far** (far, distant, remote)
`far = big dis bet thi | pla exi awa from oth pla`
*far = large distance between things | place exists away from other place*

**abo** (above, over, superior position)
`abo = pla hig tha oth pla | rel to up dir`
*above = place higher than other place | relative to upward direction*

**bel** (below, under, inferior position)
`bel = pla low tha oth pla | rel to dow dir`
*below = place lower than other place | relative to downward direction*

## Time & Change

**tim** (time, duration, temporal dimension)
`tim = whe eve hap | seq of cha sta | mea of bef aft`
*time = when events happen | sequence of changing states | measure of before after*

**now** (now, present, current moment)
`now = tim whe sen cog hap | poi bet was wil`
*now = time when sensing cognizing happen | point between was will*

**was** (past, before, anterior time)
`was = tim bef now | whe eve don hap`
*past = time before now | when events done happened*

**wil** (future, after, posterior time)
`wil = tim aft now | whe eve not yet hap`
*future = time after now | when events not yet happened*

**cha** (change, transform, alter)
`cha = tra from sta to dif sta | thi bec oth tha was`
*change = transition from state to different state | thing becomes other than was*

**tra** (transition, passage, becoming)
`tra = mov bet sta | pro of cha hap`
*transition = movement between states | process of change happening*

**lan** (language, communication system)

**say** (say, speak, utter)
`say = mak wor wth sou | sen mea to oth min`

**hea** (hear, listen, auditory sense)
`hea = sen of sou | rec wor tha oth say`
*hear = sense of sound | receive words that others say*

**ask** (ask, question, query)
`ask = say wor to get kno | req for mea from oth`
*ask = speak words to get knowledge | request for meaning from other*

**ans** (answer, reply, response)

## Operators & Compositional Concepts

**@** (projection operator)
`@ = ope tha ext par | A@B = par of A tha ali wth B | com ext`
*@ = operator that extracts parts | A@B = part of A that aligns with B | component extraction*

**^** (gradient operator)
`^ = ope for int | A^n = A wth int n | con sca from 0 to 1`
*^ = operator for intensity | A^n = A with intensity n | continuous scale from 0 to 1*

**\*** (interference operator)
`* = ope for eme | A*B = new mea from A B tog | int pat`
*\* = operator for emergence | A\*B = new meaning from A B together | interference pattern*

**\\** (subtraction operator)
`\\ = ope for rem | A\\B = A wth B par rem | sub of com`
*\\ = operator for removal | A\\B = A with B parts removed | subtraction of component*

**±** (superposition operator)
`± = ope for bot and | A±B = A B in sup | qua sta bef col`
*± = operator for both and | A±B = A B in superposition | quantum state before collapse*

**:** (conditional operator)
`: = ope for con | A:B = A giv con B | mea cha by con`
*: = operator for context | A:B = A given context B | meaning changed by context*
