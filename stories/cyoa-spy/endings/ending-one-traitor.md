```limn

```

> *Imperative: All move. Bishop enters front. Knight enters rear. Rook eyes and support. Synchronize now.*

Three assets moving as one. Your strongest play. Your most dangerous.

## FAILURE: ONE TRAITOR

**Cardinal:** Still held. Rescue failed.
**Bishop:** Captured. Sent into a trap you designed.
**Knight:** Traitor. Was never loyal. Knew everything.
**Rook:** Escaped. Alone. Burned.
**You:** Trusted the team. The team included the enemy.

You put all your assets together and one of them was poison. It spread to everything it touched.
