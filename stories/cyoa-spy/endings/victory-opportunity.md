# ENDING: Seized Moment

You sent:

```limn
we fol | dis saf | loc new | ext the | nu los the now
```

> *Imperative: Follow. Safe distance. New location. Extract there. Don't lose them now.*

Hours of patience. Now: action.

---

## Hour 21

Rook drops from the rooftop and finds a vehicle. The transfer van pulls onto the main road heading north.

```limn
roo | fol | dis 3 car | veh vis | nor on hwy | mov fas
```

> *Rook. Following. Distance: three cars. Vehicle visible. North on highway. Moving fast.*

You track from your end--cell tower handoffs, traffic camera timestamps. Two signals converging on the same northbound road.

```limn
con | cam con veh | hwy nor | kee dis | rep
```

> *Confirmed. Camera confirms vehicle. Highway north. Keep distance. Report.*

---

## Hour 22

```limn
roo | veh exi hwy | sma roa | rur are | les tra | har fol dis
```

> *Rook. Vehicle exiting highway. Small road. Rural area. Less traffic. Harder to follow at distance.*

Rook is good at this. Falls back. Uses terrain.

```limn
roo | veh tur | far roa | bui ahe | sma | old | les sec
```

> *Rook. Vehicle turning. Farm road. Building ahead. Small. Old. Less security.*

Less security. They moved Cardinal from a fortified industrial building to a farmhouse on a back road. They think they're safe now. They think no one followed.

---

## Hour 22:30

```limn
roo | pos hid | obs bui | far hou | two gua onl | car tak ins | sec min
```

> *Rook. Position hidden. Observing building. Farmhouse. Two guards only. Cardinal taken inside. Security minimal.*

You stare at the message. Two guards. Minimal security. They stripped down for transit, trading fortress walls for obscurity. They bet on not being followed.

They lost that bet.

```limn
roo | ass | two gua | nu fen | nu cam | nu ala vis | opp
```

> *Rook. Assessment. Two guards. No fence. No cameras. No alarm visible. Opportunity.*

---

## Hour 23

You plan the strike. This is what patience bought: not paralysis, but perfect timing.

```limn
roo | wai dar | gua shi cha | str the | qui ent | ext car | go
```

> *Rook. Wait for dark. Guard shift change. Strike then. Quiet entry. Extract Cardinal. Go.*

```limn
roo | con | wai | sun dow soo | red
```

> *Rook. Confirmed. Waiting. Sundown soon. Ready.*

Rook waits in the treeline. The farmhouse windows glow yellow. Two guards smoke by the front door. Inside, Cardinal sits in another room, another cell, not knowing that the long game followed them here.

---

## Hour 23:45

Darkness. The guards change. For thirty seconds, one is inside, the other walking to his car. The front door is open.

```limn
roo | now | mov | gua gap | dor ope | ins
```

> *Rook. Now. Moving. Guard gap. Door open. Inside.*

You hold your breath. The signal moves. Rook is in the farmhouse.

```limn
roo | ins | qui | hal | one dor loc | wor on it
```

> *Rook. Inside. Quiet. Hallway. One door locked. Working on it.*

---


> *Cardinal welcome home. Mission complete. Network intact. You're safe. All safe. End.*

## VICTORY: SEIZED MOMENT

**Cardinal:** Rescued from a rural safehouse with minimal security. The transfer that was meant to hide them exposed them instead.
**Rook:** Followed the vehicle for hours. Waited for darkness. Struck when the moment came.
**You:** Turned patience into action at the exact right moment. Hours of watching earned the intelligence. One command spent it.

They moved Cardinal because they thought the first location was compromised. They were right. But the move was the mistake. Fortress to farmhouse. Twelve guards to two. Cameras to darkness.

You watched. You waited. You followed. You struck.

Patience is not inaction. Patience is action waiting for its moment.
