# Spy Limn Idioms & Proverbs

Sayings that carry layered meaning in the world of espionage.

---

## Trust & Betrayal

### `tru giv | tru los | tru cos alw`
**Surface:** Trust given. Trust lost. Trust costs always.
**Depth:** The price of trust is paid whether it works or not.

### `dou saf | dou slo | dou alo`
**Surface:** Doubt is safe. Doubt is slow. Doubt is alone.
**Depth:** Paranoia keeps you alive but isolates you.

### `mol kno fir | mol die las`
**Surface:** Mole knows first. Mole dies last.
**Depth:** Double agents have information advantage but inevitable doom.

### `eye clo see mor`
**Surface:** Eyes closed see more.
**Depth:** Sometimes looking away reveals what watching hides.

## Time & Action

### `wai kil | act kil | cho kil`
**Surface:** Waiting kills. Acting kills. Choice kills.
**Depth:** There are no safe options, only different deaths.

### `tim ene alw | tim fri nev`
**Surface:** Time is enemy always. Time is friend never.
**Depth:** The clock never stops, and it's never on your side.

### `fas wro | slo wro | rig rar`
**Surface:** Fast is wrong. Slow is wrong. Right is rare.
**Depth:** Speed and caution both fail; success is precious.

### `mom pas | opp gon | sor sta`
**Surface:** Moment passes. Opportunity gone. Sorry stays.
**Depth:** Regret outlives the window it mourns.

### `key ope | key clo | key tra`

### `mes arr | mes col | mes kil`

### `iro tru | sin lie | bot sam`

## The Handler's Burden

### `you sen | the die | you sta | you kno`

**Depth:** Handlers bear the weight of distant deaths.

### `ord giv | ord fol | ord kil`

**Depth:** Command responsibility flows both ways.

### `dec fas | con slo | dec mat | con mat mor`

**Depth:** Quick choices have long shadows.

### `han nev fie | han alw fie | tru bot`

## Victory & Defeat

### `win loo los | los loo win | bor loo sam`
**Surface:** Win looks like loss. Loss looks like win. Both look same.
**Depth:** In espionage, outcomes are ambiguous.

### `mis suc | age los | vic cos`
**Surface:** Mission success. Agent lost. Victory costs.
**Depth:** Winning always has a price.

### `sur liv | sur cha | sur win te`
**Surface:** Survive. Survive changed. Survive winning?
**Depth:** Endurance transforms; is transformation victory?

### `gam end | pla con | gam nev end`
**Surface:** Game ends. Players continue. Game never ends.
**Depth:** Individual operations end; the work is eternal.

## Limn Itself

### `sup hol mea | col fix one | fix kil res`
**Surface:** Superposition holds meanings. Collapse fixes one. Fixing kills the rest.
**Depth:** Interpretation is murder of possibility.

### `key you cho | tru you mak | lie you too`
**Surface:** Key you choose. Truth you make. Lies you too.
**Depth:** Your interpretation creates reality and deception.

### `rea bef col | rea aft col | rea cha`
**Surface:** Reading before collapse. Reading after collapse. Reading changes.
**Depth:** Understanding shifts with commitment.

### `lan nev neu | wor alw wea | spe alw act`
**Surface:** Language never neutral. Words always weapons. Speaking always acting.
**Depth:** Communication is intervention.
