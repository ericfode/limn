# Word: mea

```limn
mea
```
*meaning, significance, import*

---

**Domain:** Communication / Abstract

**Core Meaning:**
The semantic content. What is signified. The weight of words.

**In Context:**

```limn
mea eme bet
```
*meaning emerges between*

```limn
mea hid | see req
```
*meaning hides. seeking required.*

```limn
nu mea | or all mea
```
*no meaning. or all meaning.*

---

Meaning is not given.
Meaning is negotiated.
Between writer and reader.
Between word and context.

```limn
mea = rel | nu abs
```
*meaning is relational. not absolute.*
