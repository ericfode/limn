## Compositional Patterns in This Scene

### 1. Anti-Aesthetic: `beu^0 * mea^0`
Zero-beauty interfered with zero-meaning creates emergent awfulness.
This is *negative composition* - the absence of qualities producing a quality.

### 2. Impossible-Actual: `pos^0 * act^1`
The signature Improbability Drive pattern. Zero possibility interfered
with maximum actuality = the impossible made real. Reusable for all
improbability events in later chapters.

### 3. Emotional Detachment: `sad^0.3`
Low gradient on emotion = alien's detached sympathy. Pattern for Ford
throughout the book: emotions at 20-40% gradient.

### 4. Physical Metaphor: `bra → liq`
Brain transforms to liquid = brain melting. The transformation operator
captures metaphorical state changes naturally.
